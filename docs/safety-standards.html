Safety & Security Standards – Outings with Friends

Outings with Friends is designed for adults to plan social meetups with people they know and trust. We take the safety of our users seriously and do not allow any content that is abusive, harmful, or illegal, including child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE).

1. Prohibited content

The app strictly prohibits:

  • Any content that sexually exploits or endangers children.
  • Any images, messages, or links involving child sexual abuse material (CSAM).
  • Harassment, threats, or incitement to violence.
  • Hate speech or other content that violates applicable laws.

2. Reporting harmful or illegal content

If you encounter harmful, abusive, or illegal content in Outings with Friends, please contact us immediately so we can review and take appropriate action. You can reach the developer at:

  • Email: theirishcavez@gmail.com

When reporting, please include as much detail as possible (screenshots, date/time, description), so we can investigate quickly.

3. Enforcement actions

In response to violations of this policy, we may:

  • Remove offending content.
  • Restrict or suspend the offending account.
  • Notify relevant service providers or app stores where appropriate.
  • Cooperate with law enforcement and other authorities where required or appropriate, particularly in cases involving children.

4. Security practices

  • All communication with our backend API uses HTTPS encryption in transit.
  • Authentication tokens are stored only on the user’s device and are not shared with others.
  • We regularly review dependencies and hosting configuration for security updates.
  • We only collect the minimum information needed to provide the service, as described in our Privacy Policy.

5. Contact

For any questions about these safety and security standards, please contact the developer at: theirishcavez@gmail.com.
